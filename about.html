Meet Our Team

President

The President oversees all club operations, ensuring that each event and initiative aligns with our mission. They represent the society in all official capacities.

Vice President

The Vice President supports the President, stepping in when necessary, and focuses on internal management, including member engagement and satisfaction.

Director of Operations

The Secretary is responsible for maintaining all records, correspondence, and ensuring effective communication within the club.

Treasurer

The Treasurer manages the club's finances, including budgeting, transactions, and financial planning, to support club activities.
